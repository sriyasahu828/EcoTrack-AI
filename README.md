🌱 EcoTrack AI

Every Choice Leaves a Footprint

EcoTrack AI is an AI-powered sustainability assistant that helps users understand and reduce their carbon footprint through interactive calculations, data visualizations, and personalized eco-friendly recommendations.

The project aims to make sustainability more accessible by transforming complex environmental data into simple insights and actionable suggestions.

---

Problem Statement

Many individuals are unaware of how their daily lifestyle choices contribute to carbon emissions. Existing carbon calculators often provide only numbers without meaningful guidance.

There is a need for an intelligent, user-friendly platform that not only measures environmental impact but also helps users make smarter and more sustainable decisions.

---

## Our Solution

EcoTrack AI combines:

* Carbon footprint calculation
* Interactive sustainability dashboard
* Emission visualization using charts
* Dynamic AI-powered suggestions
* Eco Coach for sustainability guidance
* Green points and eco badges for motivation

The platform provides personalized recommendations based on the user's transportation, electricity usage, water consumption, and food habits.

---

## Key Features

### 🌍 Carbon Footprint Calculator

Calculates carbon emissions using:

* Transportation habits
* Electricity consumption
* Water usage
* Food preferences

---

### 📊 Emission Breakdown Dashboard

Visualizes emissions through:

* Doughnut charts
* Carbon score
* Green points
* Sustainability status

Users can instantly understand which activities contribute the most to their carbon footprint.

---

### 🤖 AI Suggestions

EcoTrack AI acts as a smart assistant by generating contextual suggestions based on user input.

Examples:

* Transportation contributes 42% of your emissions.
* Try using public transport twice a week.
* Electricity usage is above average.
* Reduce AC usage by 2 hours daily.

This makes the system dynamic and user-centric instead of displaying fixed recommendations.

---

### 🌱 Eco Coach

Eco Coach is a lightweight sustainability assistant that answers user queries and provides eco-friendly recommendations related to:

* Transportation
* Electricity usage
* Food habits
* Sustainable living

---

### 🏆 Gamification

Users earn:

* Green Points
* Eco Hero badges
* Sustainability scores

This encourages long-term engagement and environmentally responsible behavior.

---

## Smart Dynamic Assistant

EcoTrack AI is designed as a context-aware assistant.

The recommendations change dynamically based on:

* Vehicle type
* Distance travelled
* Electricity consumption
* Water usage
* Food habits

This enables logical decision making and personalized sustainability guidance.
